Loan Origination System — Business Flow

Actors:

- Adaeze (Admin): Loan operations manager at QShelter
- Chidi (Customer): First-time homebuyer purchasing a 3-bedroom flat in Lekki
- Property: Lekki Gardens Estate, Unit 14B, ₦85,000,000
- Payment Plan: 10% downpayment, 90% mortgage at 9.5% p.a. over 20 years

---

1. Adaeze configures a payment plan with three phases: KYC documentation, 10% downpayment, and 20-year mortgage.

2. Chidi selects Unit 14B at Lekki Gardens and chooses the 10/90 payment plan; the system creates a draft application for him.

3. Chidi submits the application with his employment letter, bank statements, and valid ID.

4. The system evaluates Chidi's eligibility through automated underwriting and approves his application.

5. The system generates a provisional offer letter and sends it to Chidi.

6. Chidi reviews and signs the provisional offer; the signed document is stored.

7. The system generates the final offer letter with finalized terms and sends it to Chidi.

8. Chidi reviews and signs the final offer; the signed document is stored.

9. Upon Chidi's final signature, the system creates and activates his contract, scheduling the downpayment and 240 monthly mortgage installments.

10. The system sends Chidi a congratulations email with the final offer letter attached; the document is also saved to his profile.

11. Because QShelter packages this loan for Sterling Bank, the system shares the final offer letter with Sterling Bank.

12. The system notifies the servicing, accounting, and payment processing teams of Chidi's new contract.

13. All significant events and documents are retained for audit, compliance, and operational review.
